About us

Who are you?

We are a group of volunteers at Open Austin , helping making government and communities work better through the use of technology. You can see the volunteers who are contributing to this project by visiting our Github repo .

Why did you make Vax.Codes?

Everyone is trying to figure out when it is safe to gather in-person again. This project tries to help communities set up their own vaccine verification networks so that event organizers and businesses in those communities can verify if attendees and patrons have been vaccinated against Covid-19.

How can I contact your or contribute?

Since this an open source project, all you need to do is open a github issue in our project repository. You can also join our Slack channel (#p-vax-codes).

NOTE: To participate in this project, you must follow Open Austin's Code of Conduct .
